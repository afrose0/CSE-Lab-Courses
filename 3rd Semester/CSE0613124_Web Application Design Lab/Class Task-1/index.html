About My Hometown

Kushtia, a vibrant town in Bangladesh, is known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance. Home to renowned poet Rabindranath Tagore’s Shilaidaha Kuthibari and Lalon Shah’s shrine, the town reflects a deep connection to literature and mysticism. With its warm community, lush landscapes, and a blend of tradition and modernity, Kushtia stands as a symbol of history, art, and harmony
